Climate Action — SDG 13

Urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. Reduce emissions, build resilience, and empower communities worldwide.

Take Action Learn More

Why climate action matters

Climate change affects food systems, coastlines, health, and economies. SDG 13 focuses on mitigation, adaptation, and strengthened resilience—locally and globally.

1

Resilience & adaptation

Support communities to prepare for extreme weather and slow-onset impacts.

2

Policy integration

Embed climate measures into planning and national strategies.

3

Education & capacity

Improve awareness and technical skills for climate action.

Where to begin

Start with measurable steps: energy efficiency, sustainable transport, nature-based solutions, and community preparedness.

H

Household

Reduce energy use, choose efficient appliances, and reduce waste.

C

Community

Support local green audits, tree planting, and disaster-preparedness plans.

P

Policy

Advocate for science-based targets, renewable deployment, and finance for vulnerable regions.
