Practical, measurable steps individuals, communities and institutions can take to support SDG 13.

Actions Resources

Actions by scale

Actions grouped by Household, Community, and Policy scale. Each action includes simple steps and measurable outcomes.

H

Household

Switch to LED lighting, insulate homes, choose energy-efficient appliances, reduce food waste, and prefer walking or public transit. Track reductions in monthly energy bills.

C

Community

Organize green audits, community tree-planting, local resilience planning, and neighborhood renewable projects (shared solar). Measure trees planted and local energy saved.

P

Policy & Institutions

Advocate for science-based emission targets, improved public transport, nature-based solutions, and finance for vulnerable areas. Support transparent reporting and accountability.

Quick, high-impact steps

  • Reduce meat consumption and food waste.
  • Insulate buildings and improve energy efficiency.
  • Support renewable energy projects and community co-ops.
  • Vote for climate-aware leadership and policies.
