SDG 13 Targets

Summary of the main SDG 13 targets and how they translate to action at national and local levels.

See Targets Take Action

Official SDG 13 Targets (selected)

13.1

13.1 — Strengthen resilience

Improve adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards and natural disasters in all countries.

13.2

13.2 — Integrate measures

Integrate climate change measures into policies, strategies and planning across sectors.

13.3

13.3 — Education & awareness

Improve education, awareness and institutional capacity on climate change mitigation, adaptation and early warning.

13.a

13.a — Mobilize finance

Implement the commitment by developed countries to mobilize $100 billion annually for climate action in developing countries (collective goal).

13.b

13.b — Build capacity

Promote mechanisms for raising capacity for effective climate planning and management, especially in least developed countries.

Notes

National-level planning, NAPs (National Adaptation Plans), and international cooperation are central to achieving these targets.
